What Makes North Star Support Co. Different

We combine real-world human services experience with a warm, family-centered approach. Our team understands CDCS support planning not just from one angle, but from many different roles across the system. That perspective helps us communicate well, solve problems thoughtfully, and support participants with both professionalism and care.

We also believe in community impact. North Star Support Co. proudly donates a portion of revenue each quarter to local nonprofits and organizations that focus on disability advocacy and services.

Who We Are

At North Star Support Co., we believe support planning should feel clear, respectful, and genuinely helpful. Our role is to create thoughtful, person-centered support plans for individuals receiving county waivered services while making the process easier for families and partners to navigate.

We serve participants across Minnesota and understand that every person’s goals, needs, and supports are different. Our planners take the time to listen, communicate clearly, and build plans that reflect the individual behind the paperwork.
