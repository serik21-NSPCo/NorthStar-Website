FAQ

Questions about working with North Star

Here are some common questions about CDCS planning and how we work with people on disability waivers in Minnesota.

Is North Star a good fit?

North Star Planning Co may be a good fit if:

  • You or a family member is on a disability waiver in Minnesota.
  • You’d like help understanding and using CDCS options.
  • You prefer a calm, step-by-step approach instead of rushed decisions.
  • You value clear communication and collaboration with your support network.
What can I expect after I reach out?

When you submit the contact form:

  • We respond within 2 business days.
  • We may ask follow-up questions about your situation and supports.
  • We’ll discuss your specific needs and how CDCS might help.
  • Together, we’ll decide whether North Star is a good fit and outline simple next steps.
How do meetings work?

We meet in the way that works best for you:

  • Phone calls for straightforward check-ins or questions.
  • Zoom or video calls when you prefer to meet virtually.
  • In-person meetings when that’s preferred and possible.

We can include family members, guardians, or other supports in the way that feels right for you.
