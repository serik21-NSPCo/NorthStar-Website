CDCS Support Planning Services in Minnesota

North Star Support Co. helps individuals and families navigate CDCS support planning and waiver services with thoughtful guidance, responsive communication, and person-centered care.

Support Planning That Feels Clear, Respectful, and Dependable

We understand that CDCS support planning can feel overwhelming. Our role is to make the process easier to understand while creating support plans that reflect the individual’s needs, goals, and services.

What We Help With

North Star Support Co. provides Minnesota CDCS support planning services for individuals and families who need accurate, person-centered waiver planning support. We also work with case managers, referral partners, and other professionals who want a dependable support planning resource.

CDCS Support Planning

We prepare thoughtful support plans that reflect the individual’s goals, services, and support needs while helping families feel more confident throughout the process.

Waiver Planning Guidance

We help families and partners better understand the waiver planning process, what information is needed, and how to move forward in a clear and organized way.

County-Based Planner Matching

We match participants with the right planner based on county coverage so each family is connected with someone who can best support their situation.

Who We Work With

  • Individuals receiving Minnesota waiver services
  • Families navigating CDCS planning for the first time
  • Case managers and referral partners
  • Professionals looking for a responsive planning resource

Why Families Choose Us

  • Warm, person-centered communication
  • Strong understanding of support planning requirements
  • Dependable follow-through and responsiveness
  • Experienced planners serving counties across Minnesota

Our Approach to Minnesota Support Planning

We believe support planning should be more than paperwork. It should reflect the individual behind the plan. That means listening well, communicating clearly, and preparing thoughtful documentation that supports both compliance and person-centered services.

Whether someone is just getting started or has gone through the process before, our goal is to make CDCS support planning in Minnesota feel more manageable and more supportive from beginning to end.
