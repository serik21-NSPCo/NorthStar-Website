Services

CDCS planning services

Here’s a closer look at the different ways North Star Planning Co can support you with Consumer Directed Community Supports (CDCS) in Minnesota.

01 — Getting started

New CDCS plan setup

Support for participants and families who are new to CDCS or switching from traditional services. We talk through what CDCS is, what’s possible for you, and how to begin planning in a way that feels manageable.

We’ll cover what daily life looks like right now, what you’d like to be different, and how CDCS could help support those goals. You’ll leave with a clearer picture and next steps.

Great for first-time CDCS users Includes clear written summary
02 — Planning

Annual CDCS planning & updates

Collaborative planning sessions to organize goals, supports, and your CDCS budget for the year. We help you think through options, keep track of details, and adjust the plan when needs change.

This can include reviewing what has been working well, what has been hard, and changes in needs or supports. We’ll make sure the plan reflects your current situation as closely as possible.

Yearly plan + check-ins Flexible meeting options
03 — Ongoing support

Lightweight check-ins & coordination

Short, focused support throughout the year to answer questions, make small changes, or help you think through what’s next with your CDCS plan.

You can reach out when something shifts — a change in staffing, a new goal, or a new challenge — and we’ll look at how to reflect that in your plan and supports.

As-needed support Remote or in-person where possible
