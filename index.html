About

Guidance that feels steady and human

CDCS can feel confusing and overwhelming. Our role is to slow things down, help you understand options, and build a plan that fits your real life.

North Star Planning Co exists to give people on disability waivers a calmer way to navigate CDCS. You deserve clear information and steady support.

We listen first, honor your expertise in your own life, and translate CDCS rules into practical choices you can actually use.
