Professional Bio

Crystal is the founder of North Star Support Co., built on the belief that individuals and families should always be at the center of the work. In a system that can often feel overwhelming—and where consistency is rare—she is committed to providing a reliable, steady place of support. Her goal is to offer thoughtful, knowledgeable guidance that helps people navigate the entire system with confidence, not just their support plan. Crystal has worked in disability services since 2006, beginning as overnight staff supporting adolescents with Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders. That early experience sparked a deep passion for the field, which continues to shape her work today. Over the years, she has held roles in direct care, management, case management, MnCHOICES Assessments, and support planning. Her most meaningful role, however, is being a mother to her son, who has Cerebral Palsy, ADHD, and is part of the Deaf community. This lived experience, combined with her professional background, informs her compassionate, person-centered approach. Crystal is also deeply committed to advocacy and community involvement. She serves as a Family Advisor on the Guidelines Governance Council and is a member of the Family Advisory Council at Children’s Hospital and Clinics, where she has contributed since 2021. She has partnered with Gillette Children’s Specialty Healthcare through their Family Engagement in Research program, most recently working on a neuromodulation project for adults with Cerebral Palsy. She holds an Associate’s Degree in Early Childhood Education, a Bachelor’s Degree in Human Services, and a Master’s Degree in Behavioral Health from Concordia University. She is also a graduate of Partners in Policymaking (Class 41) through the Governor’s Council on Developmental Disabilities. Outside of her work, Crystal enjoys reading, podcasts, and connecting with friends through her book club and podcast group. She loves spending time with her family and pets, attending concerts and theater, and is a devoted fan of Taylor Swift. Whether you’re already part of North Star or just exploring your options, Crystal is honored to be part of your journey.
